Privacy Policy

Prism — last updated April 2026

Prism is a visual clock app designed for neurodivergent children. This policy explains how the app handles your information.

Data collection

Prism does not collect, transmit, or share any personal information. The app has no internet connection, no analytics, no advertising, and no account system.

Data stored on your device

The only data Prism stores is your app settings (such as color theme, time format, and which views are enabled). This data is saved locally on your device using Android's standard storage and is never sent anywhere.

Children's privacy

Prism is designed for use by children. Because the app collects no data of any kind, it is fully compliant with children's privacy laws including COPPA (USA) and GDPR-K (EU).

Third-party services

Prism uses no third-party services, SDKs, or libraries that collect data.

Contact

If you have any questions about this policy, please open an issue at github.com/UmbraTenebrae/nd_clock.
